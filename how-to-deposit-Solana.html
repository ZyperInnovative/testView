Wallet Binding & Deposit Guide

Secure your mining rewards by binding your Solana wallet

1

Initial Binding Prompt

A banner will automatically appear if your account doesn't have a bound wallet address. Click: "Go to Bind and Deposit" to begin the process.
2

Enter Your Wallet Address

Bind your Solana wallet address. Ensure this is the wallet you'll use for deposits, as we'll refund your total deposit during distribution.
Important: Double-check your wallet address. Transactions to incorrect addresses cannot be recovered.
3

Access Deposit Interface

Return to your dashboard and click the: "Receive" button to access your deposit address.
4

Copy Deposit Address

Click "Copy Address" to copy your unique Solana deposit address to clipboard.
5

Send Initial Deposit

Send ≥$5 worth of SOL to cover:
  • Mining gas fees
  • Transaction costs
  • Human verification
  • Anti-spam measures
This initial deposit will be refunded during distribution along with your mining rewards. Make sure to use the wallet address you bound to your account when depositing.
6

Verify Your Transaction

Return to the dashboard and click the "Receive" button again, then scroll down to find the verification section.
7

Complete Verification

In the "Sol Deposit Verification" section, click "Verify Transaction" to confirm your deposit.
✓

Deposit Complete!

Your deposits will now be automatically counted. You can track your balance and mining progress on your dashboard.
